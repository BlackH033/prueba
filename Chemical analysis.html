SOIL CHEMICAL ANALYSIS




Soil chemical analysis measures nutritional levels in the soil. It is a diagnostic and guidance tool that should be considered together with available information on soil characterization, productivity potential, crop and management history, as well as the human factor. However, its use is not really widespread in the agricultural sector and even less so in livestock or forestry farms. Through the regular use of soil analysis it is possible to monitor the nutritional status and nutritional changes occurring in the soil, in order to maintain its productivity.
